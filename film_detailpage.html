SYNOPSIS
Anora, a young woman from Brooklyn,
gets her chance at a Cinderella story when she meets
and impulsively marries the son of an oligarch.
Once the news reaches Russia,
her fairytale is threatened as the parents set out for
New York to get the marriage annulled.
